Your Rights
Residential Tenancies Act 1997 — Victoria
Landlords must ensure properties meet minimum standards at all times. Urgent repairs must be attended to within 24 hours (emergency) or 14 days (urgent). Routine repairs should be completed within a reasonable time.

Consumer Affairs Victoria: consumer.vic.gov.au · 1300 55 81 81
Victorian Civil and Administrative Tribunal (VCAT): vcat.vic.gov.au

Repairs Timeframes — Victoria
What to expect
Emergency repairs (gas leaks, no power, security): within 24 hours
Urgent repairs (hot water, essential appliances): within 3 days
Routine repairs (non-urgent maintenance): within 14 days or a reasonable time
